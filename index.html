Meet Raquel

A 25-year career built on
trust, results, and real Spanish

Raquel Alonso Gonzalez is a native Spanish speaker and professional instructor with over 25 years of experience teaching Spanish at the highest levels — including to U.S. diplomats, USAID professionals, and foreign service officers at the U.S. Department of State's Foreign Service Institute who need real, functional fluency for their work around the world.

"Spanish is more than a language — it's a door. My job is to help you open it with confidence, whether you're a complete beginner or preparing for your next diplomatic posting."

Based in Rockville, Maryland, Raquel teaches in-person locally and online to students across the U.S. and internationally — from Maryland to Mexico and beyond. Her approach is patient, structured, and always tailored to you: your level, your goals, your pace.

Raquel teaches students of all ages — elementary school through adult professionals. If you've never spoken a word of Spanish, you're in exactly the right place.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need any Spanish to get started? +
Not at all — complete beginners are warmly welcome. Raquel starts with a free consultation to understand exactly where you are and what you'd like to achieve.
How long are sessions and how often? +
Standard sessions are 60 minutes. Most students take 1–2 lessons per week. Raquel will recommend a cadence based on your goals.
Are lessons online or in-person? +
Both. Online via Zoom is available to students anywhere in the world. In-person lessons are available in and around Rockville, MD.
What ages does Raquel teach? +
Elementary-age students through adult professionals. Every lesson is adapted to the learner's age, attention span, and goals.
Can I reschedule or cancel lessons? +
Yes — with 24 hours notice you can reschedule at no charge. Monthly plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment.
Which exams does Raquel prepare students for? +
AP Spanish Language & Culture, DELE (A1–C2), SIELE, school finals, and university placement exams.
Which variety of Spanish is taught? +
Both Latin American and Castilian Spanish, tailored to whichever is most useful for your goals — travel, school, or professional work.
Is the free trial lesson really free? +
Yes — completely free, no pressure, no obligation. A chance to meet Raquel, discuss your goals, and see if it's the right fit.
